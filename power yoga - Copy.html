Power Yoga

By practicing yoga poses you achieve a better emotional stability, the absence of feelings of deprivation and destruction, self-satisfaction, finding the purpose of life, getting to know your own way of reacting - through directing your attention to the processes in your body, slow and deep breaths, harmony of breathing and movement, physical and mental relaxation. That way you also encourage focusing on present, groundedness in reality, and a hopeful view of the future. Hatha Yoga is ideal for beginners, as well as advanced fitness enthusiasts, and is recommended to all those who are in need of harmony and balance in their lives.
