Yoga

What do we achieve by doing yoga?

By doing yoga our body becomes more flexible, and we're improving vitality of our spine as well as the ankle-joint system. These are very important points that make yoga stand out from other disciplines. One of the most important yet most neglected things is breathing. We pay special attention to breathing during workout. By breathing properly we improve functionality of all our internal organs and take in a greater amount of oxygen that is needed in order to feed every cell. By doing yoga, we strengthen our will, determination, and endurance, we find peace and connect to the love within us. We stimulate the sympathetic and parasympathetic nervous system, boost our concentration, and normalize the functions of our organs and glands of internal secretion. Therefore, the number of doctors who recommend practicing yoga is increasing, since it provides numerous health benefits. Yoga is a lifestyle that implies nurturing your body, emotions, and mind every day. It is a discipline where we change ourselves and make our life more beautiful, harmonious, and healthier by a single effort. Yoga doesn't isolate a single aspect of our being, we are one - physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual. Whatever is going on in our mind is happening to our body as well and therefore it is important that there is harmony.

Let's do yoga for a better youth and a healthier old age!
